NEW PLANET HOTEL & SUITES

Committed to provide only the highest levels of comfort, quality accommodations and conference facilities has made New Planet Hotels & Suites ideal for leisure, corporate travelers, stopovers & long stays. Each and every stay at one of our properties offers a high level of warmth and art of hospitality. We have an extensive choice of accommodations to choose from with a portfolio including standard hotel rooms, deluxe hotel suites, studios, Royal Suites, Executive & Privilege Floors.

Luxury Resort

New Planet Hotel and Suites epitomizes opulence and relaxation. Nestled in a picturesque location, this lavish retreat offers guests a taste of unparalleled comfort and indulgence. With meticulously designed accommodations, world-class amenities, gourmet dining, and impeccable service, it's the ultimate destination for those seeking a lavish escape from the everyday hustle and bustle. Whether you're unwinding by the pristine pool, indulging in spa treatments, or savoring delectable cuisine, the Luxury Resort at New Planet Hotel promises an unforgettable, upscale experience for the discerning traveler.

Wedding Venue

The event hall at New Planet Hotel and Suites is a dream setting for couples seeking a romantic and memorable celebration. With elegant event spaces, customizable decor options, and a dedicated team of professionals, it's the ideal place to exchange vows and celebrate with loved ones. Whether you envision an intimate ceremony or a grand reception, the Wedding Venue at New Planet Hotel ensures that your wedding day is a truly magical experience, creating cherished memories that will last a lifetime.
